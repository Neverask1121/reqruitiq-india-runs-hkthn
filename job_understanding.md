# Job Analysis

## Project

**Redrob AI – Intelligent Candidate Discovery & Ranking Challenge**

---

# 1. Job Overview

## Position

**Senior AI Engineer – Founding Team**

## Company

Redrob AI (Series A AI-native Talent Intelligence Platform)

## Location

Hybrid (Pune / Noida)

Candidates willing to relocate from Tier-1 Indian cities are also considered.

## Experience

The job description mentions **5–9 years** of experience, but explicitly states that this is **not a strict requirement**. The company prioritizes **quality of experience, ownership, and production impact** over the number of years worked.

---

# 2. Role Summary

The selected engineer will own the intelligence layer of Redrob's hiring platform.

The primary responsibilities include:

* Designing candidate ranking systems
* Building retrieval and search systems
* Improving candidate-job matching
* Deploying AI models into production
* Measuring and improving ranking quality

The role requires balancing strong AI expertise with practical product development.

# 8. Hidden Requirements (Important AI Insights)

The job description intentionally includes several implicit expectations.

### Builder Mindset

The ideal candidate is expected to build practical solutions quickly rather than focusing only on perfect research.

### Product Thinking

Candidates should understand recruiter workflows and user problems, not just machine learning algorithms.

### Production Experience

Real-world deployment experience is significantly more valuable than academic research.

### System Design

The company values engineers who understand complete AI systems rather than individual frameworks.

### Learning Ability

Candidates should be comfortable adapting as company priorities evolve.

---

# 9. Negative Signals (Candidates to Down-Rank)

The company explicitly discourages candidates with the following profiles:

* Research-only background without production deployment
* Only recent LLM or LangChain experience
* No recent production coding experience
* Entire career limited to service-based consulting companies without product experience
* Primary expertise only in Computer Vision, Robotics, or Speech without NLP or Information Retrieval
* Framework-focused developers without strong system design knowledge
* Candidates primarily chasing job titles instead of long-term impact

These should reduce the ranking score.

---

# 10. Ideal Candidate Profile

The strongest candidate is likely to have:

* 6–8 years of relevant experience
* Product company background
* Experience building search, recommendation, or ranking systems
* Strong knowledge of embeddings and retrieval
* Strong evaluation methodology
* Excellent Python skills
* Startup mindset
* Willingness to relocate to Pune or Noida
* Active job seeker with positive recruiter engagement

---

# 11. Important Insight for the Ranking System

The hackathon specifically instructs participants **not** to rely on keyword matching.

The ranking model should evaluate:

* Real project experience
* Career progression
* Production deployments
* Semantic relevance
* Behavioral signals
* Candidate availability
* Recruiter engagement
* Practical engineering impact

A candidate with genuine production experience but fewer AI buzzwords should rank higher than a candidate with many AI keywords but unrelated work experience.

---

# 12. Key Takeaways for the Team

The ranking system should prioritize:

1. Production AI experience
2. Retrieval and ranking expertise
3. Product company experience
4. Strong Python engineering skills
5. Evaluation methodology
6. Builder mindset
7. Semantic relevance instead of keyword matching
8. Behavioral signals indicating hiring readiness

This analysis will serve as the foundation for the scoring strategy and ranking engine used throughout the project.
